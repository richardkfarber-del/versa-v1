# Radiant Sanctuary (Versa Velvet) Design System
**Do not deviate or invent new colors.**

## Color Palette
* Background: Deep Midnight (`#0e0e0e`)
* Primary Accent: Jewel Plum (`#f183ff`)
* Secondary Accent: Saffron (`#ff9800`)
* Surfaces: `surface-container-high` on focus.

## Typography
* Headlines: `Manrope`
* Body Text: `Plus Jakarta Sans`

## Effects & Spacing
* Glassmorphism: `backdrop-blur-2xl` (40px blur, 20% transparent surface)
* Corner Radius (Buttons): `1.5rem`
* Vertical Spacing (Forms): `2.75rem`
