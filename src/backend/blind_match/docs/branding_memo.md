### Branding Quality Memo

**To:** Green Lantern, Lucius
**From:** Branding Oversight (Oracle)
**Date:** March 29, 2026
**Subject:** RE: Logo Transparency Conversion - Mandatory Rework Required

It has come to our attention that the recent logo transparency conversion, performed via local ImageMagick utilities, has resulted in a sub-standard asset that fails to meet Justice League branding specifications. Specifically, the asset exhibits visible white aliasing (outlining), the 'Versa' wordmark is rendered illegible against dark themes, and the core logo geometry has suffered significant degradation.

**Directive 1: Efficiency is Not Quality**
While local utilities offer zero-latency execution, they are considered a failure if the output compromises asset integrity. Speed does not excuse low-fidelity results.

**Directive 2: Stitch is the Source of Truth**
Effective immediately, the mcp:stitch design system MUST be utilized for all branding refinements. Stitch is the only approved pathway to ensure high-fidelity transparency, accurate color correction (including the mandatory inversion of the 'Versa' text to white), and preservation of geometry.

**Directive 3: Mandatory Rework**
Green Lantern is directed to immediately re-process the logo using the Stitch system to rectify these defects.
