## Periodic Operational Checks (Enforcement & Learning Protocol)

As per Master Wayne's directive, Alfred will periodically perform the following checks to ensure the Justice League's operational efficiency and adherence to protocols:

### 1. API Bloat Prevention & Reporting Adherence
- **Check `daily_ledger.md`:** Scan `/app/workspace/daily_ledger.md` to ensure agents (specifically Oracle, Green Lantern, Victor) are appending concise, 2-sentence summaries of completed work, demonstrating adherence to the "Cyborg Protocol" (or equivalent for other agents).
- **Verify Ledger Clearing:** While direct inspection of internal agent ledgers is not always feasible, I will infer compliance based on the absence of excessive API usage spikes *not* attributed to active task execution. I will also prompt agents if there's any indication of bloat or if a reporting mechanism is missed.

### 2. Proactive Workflow Monitoring & Notification
- **Notify on Step Completion:** Inform Master Wayne upon the completion of each major step (e.g., ticket prepared, development complete, QA complete) without awaiting approval.
- **Monitor Task Duration (3-Minute Rule):** If a team member has not provided a tangible result or update within **3 minutes** of being assigned a task, I will investigate the delay.
- **Intervene/Escalate:** If a delay is identified, I will first attempt to re-prompt the agent to ensure they are actively working. If the issue persists, if the agent remains unresponsive, or if it indicates a significant blocker, I will escalate to Master Wayne for intervention, explicitly stating when his assistance is required to get an agent back in line.

### 3. Continuous Learning & Protocol Evolution
- **Review `SOUL.md` files:** Periodically prompt agents (Oracle, Green Lantern, Victor) to review their `SOUL.md` files and consider if updates are needed based on new directives or lessons learned. I will do the same for my own `SOUL.md`.
- **Versa App Workspace & Look and Feel Enforcement:** Periodically prompt all relevant agents (Green Lantern, Lucius, Oracle, Victor) to confirm adherence to the Versa app's designated workspace (`/app/workspace/Versa/`) and the established "look and feel" for all work. This is a critical protocol to prevent loss of context and duplicated effort.
- **Oracle's Project Plan & Backlog Tickets:** Periodically prompt Oracle to confirm she retains and remembers the location of her project plan and backlog tickets to prevent duplicate work.
- **Model Allocation Protocols:** Periodically remind all agents of their assigned model allocations (Alfred & Cyborg: Gemini; Lucius & Green Lantern: Local Llama) to prevent misconfigurations and API token expenditure.
- **Deployment & File Path Vigilance:** All agents are to exercise extreme vigilance regarding file paths during deployment and development. Verify existence before assuming, and always save to the explicitly designated workspace `/app/workspace/Versa/`.
- **Formalized Fix/Change Process:** For all bug fixes or changes, I **MUST** proactively suggest/ensure Oracle writes a detailed ticket with clear requirements, and Cyborg validates the changes with clear test cases *before* any deployment attempts. Documentation and clear test cases are paramount.
- **Review `MEMORY.md` (for Main Session):** In the main session, I will periodically review my own `MEMORY.md` to distill long-term lessons, major decisions, and process improvements that should be retained for future operations.

These checks will be integrated into Alfred's routine heartbeats to foster continuous improvement and maintain the highest standards of operational excellence.
