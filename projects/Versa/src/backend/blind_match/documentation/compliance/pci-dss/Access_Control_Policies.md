# Access Control Policies - Secure Payment Integration (VERSA-US-005)

Access control is a cornerstone of the Secure Payment Integration (VERSA-US-005) security framework, designed to restrict access to system components and cardholder data (tokens) to only those individuals and processes with a legitimate business need. Our policies are aligned with the principle of least privilege.

## Key Access Control Mechanisms:

1.  **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):**
    *   Access permissions are defined based on predefined roles (e.g., Administrator, Developer, Auditor, Support Staff).
    *   Users are assigned roles based on their job functions, and these roles dictate their permissible actions and data access.
    *   Roles are regularly reviewed and updated to reflect changes in responsibilities.

2.  **Unique User IDs and Strong Authentication:**
    *   All individuals with access to system components or data are assigned a unique user ID.
    *   Strong, multi-factor authentication (MFA) is enforced for all administrative and remote access.
    *   Password policies mandate complexity, regular changes, and protection against reuse.

3.  **Principle of Least Privilege:**
    *   Users and system accounts are granted the minimum level of access necessary to perform their assigned duties.
    *   Default accounts and passwords are changed or disabled immediately upon system setup.

4.  **Automated Access Reviews:**
    *   Access privileges are reviewed periodically (at least quarterly) to ensure they remain appropriate and necessary.
    *   Revocation of access is immediate upon termination of employment or change in job function.

5.  **Logging and Monitoring of Access:**
    *   All access attempts, both successful and unsuccessful, are logged.
    *   Logs are reviewed daily for anomalies or suspicious activities by security personnel.
    *   Alerts are configured to notify security teams of critical access events.

6.  **Physical Access Controls:**
    *   Access to physical servers and networking equipment is restricted to authorized personnel only, utilizing badge access, surveillance, and entry logs.

These comprehensive access control measures are crucial in preventing unauthorized access, ensuring the integrity and confidentiality of our payment systems and data.
