# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Product: Smart Home Energy Management System (SHEMS)

### 1. Market Analysis
The Smart Home Energy Management System (SHEMS) market is experiencing rapid growth, driven by rising global electricity costs, increased adoption of EV/renewable energy (solar, home batteries), and shifting grid policies (Time-of-Use pricing). 
*   **Target Audience:** Eco-conscious homeowners, early adopters of smart home tech, and cost-sensitive consumers looking to reduce monthly bills.
*   **Competitor Landscape:** Ranges from single-device optimizers (e.g., Google Nest, Ecobee) to comprehensive whole-home hardware solutions (e.g., Sense, Span smart panels, Tesla Powerwall). 
*   **Market Gap:** There is a significant opportunity for software-first, hardware-agnostic platforms that integrate disparate smart devices (thermostats, EV chargers, smart plugs) into a single, automated cost-saving ecosystem without requiring an expensive electrical panel upgrade.

### 2. Core Features (MVP)
To deliver immediate value, the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will focus on visibility, basic automation, and cost-saving insights.

#### 2.1. Real-Time Energy Dashboard
*   **Feature:** A centralized mobile/web view of current and historical energy consumption (kWh).
*   **Value:** Provides immediate visibility into whole-home energy draw and daily/weekly trends.

#### 2.2. Time-of-Use (TOU) Rate Integration
*   **Feature:** Users can input or sync their utility company's TOU rate schedule.
*   **Value:** Translates kWh usage into actual financial costs, highlighting expensive peak hours.

#### 2.3. Smart HVAC & High-Draw Appliance Scheduling
*   **Feature:** Integrates with popular smart thermostats (e.g., Nest, Ecobee) and smart plugs to automatically pre-cool/pre-heat the home or run appliances during off-peak, low-cost hours.
*   **Value:** Delivers direct, automated financial savings without requiring daily manual intervention.

#### 2.4. Budgeting and Anomaly Alerts
*   **Feature:** Push notifications for unusual energy spikes (e.g., HVAC running continuously) or when the user is projected to exceed their monthly energy budget.
*   **Value:** Prevents "bill shock" at the end of the month.

#### 2.5. Basic Usage Categorization
*   **Feature:** Categorizes energy usage (e.g., "Always On", "Heating/Cooling", "Other") either via basic estimation algorithms or direct smart plug integration.
*   **Value:** Helps users identify "energy vampires" in their home.

### 3. Future Roadmap (Post-MVP)
*   EV Charger optimization (charging only when rates are lowest).
*   Solar inverter and home battery integration.
*   AI-driven, weather-based predictive HVAC adjustments.
