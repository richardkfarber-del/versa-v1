### 🎫 TICKET: MVP-UI-003
**Title:** Active Date Night Screen
**Assignee:** Green Lantern
**Priority:** High / P1
**App:** Versa (MVP)

**User Story:**
As a user, I want a timer-based, synced interface to guide me and my partner through a 15-minute activity, so we can focus on each other without distractions.

**Objective:**
Design the "Active Date Night" interface. This screen appears after selecting a shared activity from the Match Reveal. It should act as a clean, distraction-free environment that syncs a timer between both partners.

**Design Requirements & Scope (MVP):**
- **Aesthetic:** "Headspace meets a luxury spa". Safe, calming, intimate, and modern. No distractions.
- **Color Palette:** Deep, soothing jewel tones or dark mode optimized (midnight backgrounds) to reduce harsh light.
- **Typography & Imagery:** Clean sans-serif. Soft ambient animations or minimalist timers.
- **Key Elements:**
  - Clear, prominent 15-minute countdown timer (synchronized).
  - The name of the selected activity.
  - A prominent, always-visible "Red Light" / Safe Word button (trauma-informed safety feature).
  - A subtle "Pause" or "Extend Time" option.

**Deliverables Required:**
- High-fidelity mockups for the Active Date Night screen.
- A technical PRD detailing state management (timer syncing, Safe Word trigger).
- Component scaffolding.
