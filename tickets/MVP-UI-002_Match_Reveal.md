### 🎫 TICKET: MVP-UI-002
**Title:** The Match Reveal Screen
**Assignee:** Green Lantern
**Priority:** High / P1
**App:** Versa (MVP)

**User Story:**
As a user, I want to see only the activities where both my partner and I answered "Yes" or "Maybe" so we can focus on shared desires without feeling rejected or exposed.

**Objective:**
Design the "Match Reveal" interface. This screen appears after both partners complete the Connection Compass. It should act as a soft, celebratory reveal of shared interests.

**Design Requirements & Scope (MVP):**
- **Aesthetic:** "Headspace meets a luxury spa". Safe, dreamy, modern, and high-end. Explicitly avoid standard dating/porn app tropes.
- **Color Palette:** Vibrant jewel tones against midnight or clean off-white backgrounds.
- **Typography & Imagery:** Clean, modern sans-serif fonts. Use abstract fluid shapes, inclusive line-art, or soft-focus ambient photography. 
- **Key Elements:**
  - A celebratory but soothing header (e.g., "Your Shared Desires").
  - A visually pleasing list or grid of matched activities.
  - A clear CTA to proceed to the "Active Date Night" phase.
